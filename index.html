Alpha Terminal

Alpha Terminal is VPet Data Extraction Utility Application.

Using an Alpha-Com (or compatible VPet Interface Alpha-Com, D-Com, and the Digivice Helper are all Compatible VPet Interfaces. ), connect your VPet device and scan The SCAN function extracts data from your VPet device to generate a DigiROM the data within, or send Providing a DigiROM and using the SEND function, your VPet will interact with the contents. (E.g. battle, jogress, copy, trade) data to your device to battle, jogress, copy, or trade!
Available on Android, Windows, Linux, and MacOS, Alpha Terminal uses DigiROMs Digital Read-Only-Memory - The DigiROM contains data extracted from a VPet to connect you with other VPet users around the world. After obtaining a DigiROM from your VPet, share it with your friends so they can send it to their device. Join the 0NL1NE Community to find others to connect with!

Alpha Link

Integrating the VPet Interfacing technology from Alpha Terminal, the Alpha Link app introduces storage and social features.

Creating an account and joining Alpha Link gives you a place to store the Digimon you raise on your VPet, and send them out to challenge others. As the Alpha Eco-System grows, you will be able to access your Alpha Link account in other Alpha applications.
